Enzo Gorlomi of Papa's


Image source

Enzo Gorlomi is an Italian chef and owner of Papa's. Enzo has transformed the world of pizza with his passion and expertise.

Born in Naples, Enzo's culinary journey began in his grandmother's kitchen, and today, he stands as a master of Neapolitan-style cuisine.

With a dedication to authenticity and quality, Enzo has solidified his place as a beloved figure in the world of Italian cuisine.

Purpose, goals and audience

The purpose and goals of Papa's is to inform new customers about Papa's; what Papa's is and what Papa's offers, as well as acting as a link between hungry customers and the cooks.

The website will show customers the rich menu and the golden history, and the cozy essence of Papa's through our hand-crafted design. We will have a dedicated page to Papa's menu, include Enzo's and Papa's lovely and rich backstory, as well as a contact form to get in touch with Papa's.

Papa's audience consists mainly of young adults, couples, and families looking for that little extra.

Users would want to find and read about menu items; their history, their allergens, their ingredients, and the price. Some users may want to contact Papa's; be it for reserving a table for their whole family, or asking for their lost jacket. Other users may want to read about Papa's history and the culture surrounding it.

Navigation Structure

Our navigation structure will be very simple as our project is for a restaurant. Every page will have access to every page with the exception of the order form page. That way it will be easy for our customers to navigate wherever they want whenever they want. This makes the most sense as our website will not have any private pages or any pages in a hierarchy. You do not need to visit "about us" and read about the restaurants history before you check out the menu. Those who want to read more about our website will do that, those who just want to see the menu will do that. The order form page is hierarchically underneath the order page as everyone who wants to order should read the info at the order page before sending in an order form.

Page Layout and Appearance

When coming up with the design, we were focused on giving the users the impression of a warm and cozy restaurant. We therefore decided to use a lot of warm and dark tones.In other words, the color scheme is composed of different shades of orange and black. At the same time we want to make sure the page is simple and easy to use. The navigation bar is located at the top of the page. Access to all tabs lie in the navigation bar, so it'll be easy for customers to navigate on the page. The footer includes copyright information, as well as contact information and the address. The nav bar and footer uses the color #F2C063, the background uses the color #FAE2B7 and any elements on the page will use #FDD78F. All text and and icons will use the color #1E1E1E. The Papa's logo will use the font Berkshire Swash, the page titles will use the font Jolly lodger and all other text will use the font Lexend.

Content

Homepage

The homepage's purpose is to welcome the visitor into the warm atmosphere of Papa's restaurant. To accomplish this goal, we decided to add a grand picture of Papa's restaurant along the beautiful landscape next to it to further interest the visitor. We also added a welcome message along our motto(?) to greet the visitor, because everyone is welcome at Papa's! Obviously, Papa's is not all words and empty promises; We want our client to experience the wonderful taste of Italian cuisine! As such we include three links; one that directs the user to our menu-page, one to direct the user to our about us page, and one to direct the user to make-an-order page, all the aforementioned pages will be discussed further down in this document.

Menu-page

Our Menu-page is designed to be easily readable and have a clear and crisp design. The page is divided into two sections: one for our pizzas and one for our pastas. We plan to add further menu items to the website in the future, especially a drink section. The website then explains a little about our preparation process for those who are interested. We keep the description short and concise so as not to overwhelm the visitor with too much information; In the end we want our would-be customer to get to taste our great work and not just read about it! All our pizzas and pastas are placed within their own boxes with the name of the pizza, a brief description, list of ingredients and allergens, and a more thorough history/about paragraph for those who are interested. We decided to present our dishes in this manner to make them stand out such that the page-visitors can easily find what they want to eat and order it swiftly! This page fulfills the goal of showing the menu to our visitors

Order-now page

Our order-now page functions as a lead-in page to the place-your-order page and as such it's very simple in design. The visitor is greeted with an image of one of our recognisable pizza-mopeds, while the text informs the user about our process of delivery and how long it's expected to take. The ‚Äúplace an order‚Äù button takes the user directly to the place-your-order page.

Place-your-order page

Our place-your-order page is very simple in design: there are empty fields that need to be filled in by the customer. We ask for their full name, address, phone number, email address, the food items and the drink items. The user can then choose to send the order, which will take them to a webpage where they fill out the payment details (not a part of our website), or to go back to the order now page from which they can navigate to other sections of the website through the navigation bar or footer. We also purposefully include a picture of an item from our menu to make the would-be customer more appetised.

Contact

The contact page will include all the contact information related to Papa's. This page will fulfill the goal of informing the customers of Papa's about the different ways to contact the restaurant. The layout is simple, with three boxes containing each piece of contact information. Each of these boxes will have a width of 1150px and a height of 750px. Contact us page The contact us page functions as a way of providing the user with the most crucial information about our restaurant. This includes a box with information about when we take calls and our number, another box that contains our email information; one email for general inquiries and another one for making reservations, and a final box that contains a link to our Facebook for information and updates. We chose to include a Facebook link since social media branding is important for gaining a customer base.

About us page

The about us page contains info about our restaurant's history, the traditions we hold so dearly, our food, and who Enzo Gorlomi is. The page is divided into 4 sections with text and image of every section. A different approach to the about us page would be to have it as a part of our main page and instead let the user scroll down to reveal its content. However we chose this approach because we believe it's clearer and easier to understand for the average user; our main goal is always to accommodate our customers!
